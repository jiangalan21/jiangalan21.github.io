Since this architecture excites you, here are three specific terms to Google or ask me about as you continue your journey:
* gRPC: A way for servers to talk to each other much faster than standard JSON/REST. It’s what Google and Netflix use for their internal "phone calls."

* Kubernetes (K8s): The "Boss" of the Containers. If you have 500 containers, Kubernetes is the software that manages them all.

* Control Plane vs. Data Plane: This is the formal name for what we just discussed (The "Brain" vs. the "Video Cache"). Learning this terminology will help you ace high-level system design interviews.
